Apprenticeship Program

Benefits

  • Earn while you learn. As an apprentice, you will receive a salary from the employer who hires you.
  • Lower debt. No College debt!
  • Gain work experience. Skilled workers are in demand all across the country.
  • Benefits from qualified mentors.

Objectives

By the end of the Apprenticeship, delegates will be able to:

  • Fit tyres of all shapes and sizes on car/van, truck and trailer, and agri tyres
  • Repair damage to tyres and fit and balance new tyres to vehicles.
  • Inspect a vehicle's tyres and the related components, checking for signs of damage and wear
  • Work both on your own and as part of a team
  • Understand the health & Safety procedures when changing, inflating and working with tyres

Roadside Recovery:

  • Understand the health & Safety procedures for roadside breakdowns
  • Demonstrate the correct use of all equipment necessary for roadside breakdowns
  • Understand the different roadside procedures required between motorway and non motorway roads

Content

  • Fitting of Truck, car/Van and Agricultural Tyres
  • Health and Safety Procedures
  • Roadside recovery:Motorway, non-motorway and single carriageway procedures
  • Puncture and damage repair
  • Retorque procedure
  • Tools needed and their maintenance
  • tracking, balancing & regrooving
  • fleet management & tyre surveying
  • Emergency procedures and first aid

Roadside Breakdown & Safety Course

Benefits

The Roadside Breakdown course is essential for technicians carrying out commercial tyre fitting or wheel changing at the roadside and have yet to receive formal training. On passing the course it will provide them with an industry recognised accreditation and offer peace of mind to employers and customers that the technician is aware of the safe working practices when attending Roadside breakdowns.

Objectives

  • Understand the health & Safety procedures for roadside breakdowns
  • Demonstrate the correct use of all equipment necessary for roadside breakdowns
  • Understand the different roadside procedures required between motorway and non motorway roads

Content

  • Pre-breakdown planning list of tasks
  • Approaching the breakdown site health & Safety precautions
  • Motorway and non motorway procedures
  • Completion procedures
  • Tools needed and their maintenance
  • Emergency procedures and first aid
